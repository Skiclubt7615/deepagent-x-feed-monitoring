## ⚙️ How to Use deepagent-x-feed-monitoring

After you open the program, you will see a simple window that shows your X feed updates.

### Basic steps to monitor your feed:

- Enter your X username or handle into the main field.  
- Click **Start Monitoring** to begin watching your feed.  
- New posts will appear in the list below as they happen.  
- You can pause the feed at any time by clicking **Pause**.  
- Use the settings menu to adjust how often the program checks for new posts.
